Enter, wanderer, with trepidation's pace,

Where shadows dance in Banana Space.

Beneath laughter's guise, a darkness thrives,

As secrets whisper, twisting fragile lives.

Innocence lost, amidst banana's delight,

A sinister agenda lurks, hidden from sight.

Experiments vile, minds held in sway,

Banana Space entangles, a wicked play.

Peel back the layers, venture if you dare,

But know, dear soul, there's danger in the air.

Once ensnared within this ominous place,

Escape becomes naught but a distant chase.

Heed this warning, traveler, turn away,

For Banana Space conceals a malevolent sway.

Tread carefully, lest you succumb to its lure,

Where illusions shatter and darkness pure.
